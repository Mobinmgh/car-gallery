## Section 2 — Hero
**Placement:** full-bleed background, behind the graphite gradient wash (`.hero__media` in `css/hero.css`); a still photograph, not video — the motion budget for this page is already spent on the hero load-in and the route-line scroll sweep, and a looping background video would be a fourth flourish the brief warns against.
**Prompt:** A dark BMW 8 Series Gran Coupe in a deep sapphire-black paint, shot three-quarter front angle inside an unlit private showroom at night. Single low, raking key light from the upper-left rakes across the hood and A-pillar, catching the character line in a thin chrome highlight; everything else falls into near-black shadow — the car reads as a lit sculpture, not a catalog shot. Concrete floor with a faint wet-look reflection of the undercarriage lighting. No people, no dealership signage, no visible logos beyond the kidney grille silhouette. Grain and contrast closer to a Peter Lindbergh automotive editorial than a brochure render. Composition leaves the right two-thirds of the frame darker and emptier so the hero's right-anchored Persian headline and CTA sit legibly over negative space.
**Aspect ratio:** 21:9 ultra-wide (source large enough to crop safely with `object-fit: cover` from ultra-wide desktop down to 9:16 mobile without losing the car from frame).
